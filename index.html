Century vs Whitney Var. Boys Basketball

Game Summary and Highlights

Whitney came out strong and with intensity.

Century, however, was a weak team compared to Whitney which allowed Whitney to capitalize on Century's uncoordinated defense and slow offensive pace.

Whitney was knocking down shots and getting easy fast breaks while locking in on defense.

By halftime, we built an impressive lead of 28-6

Although Whitney continued to lead through passing, defense, and consistent shooting, Century began to make their shots and make difficult layups and and-ones.

Despite this, Whitney pulled through by continuing playing with heart and skill ending with a victory and closing the game out with a 54-28 victory.
